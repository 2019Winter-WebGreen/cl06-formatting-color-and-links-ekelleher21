Labradoodles are the Best

Why labradoodles are the best types of dogs!

A Labradoodle is a crossbreed dog created by crossing the Labrador retriever and the Standard, Medium, or Miniature poodle.

They are intelligent, friendly and moderately active. Some labradoodles can be hyperallergenic. They are easier to train than other dogs and they tend to love their people and be devoted to thier families.
